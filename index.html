About Me

Driven by a mission to revolutionize transportation on Earth and beyond, I am the founder and CEO of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., co-founder of Neuralink, and founder of The Boring Company. My work focuses on advancing humanity through sustainable energy, interplanetary travel, and innovative technology.

  • SpaceX: Making life multiplanetary.
  • Tesla: Accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy.
  • Neuralink: Merging the human brain with AI.
  • The Boring Company: Solving urban traffic with tunnels.
